My Dashboards

Book Explorer Dashboard

This dashboard allows users to search for books and visualize their genre distribution. It fetches book data from the **Google Books API**. Users can enter a title or author to get a list of relevant books. The results are presented with details like author, cover image, and a truncated description.

A key feature is the interactivity: users can click on any book card to open a new tab with the full book details on Google Books. Additionally, the dashboard provides a "Load More" option to fetch more results beyond the initial set, and a pie chart dynamically displays the distribution of book genres based on the search results.

Space Explorer Dashboard

Dive into the cosmos with this dashboard, featuring real-time astronomical data. This dashboard integrates with **NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) API** and **NASA's Mars Rover Photos API**.

The **Astronomy Picture of the Day** section updates daily, showcasing a new cosmic image or video with a detailed explanation. Users can also select past dates to explore the rich history of APOD entries.

The **Mars Rover Photos** section allows users to browse recent images taken by NASA's Mars rovers. Users can select different rovers and even specific cameras to filter photos from the Martian surface. This provides a fascinating glimpse into humanity's exploration of the Red Planet.
